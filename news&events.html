Pongal Celebration

The vibrant spirit of Pongal celebration echoed through The Nest School, as learners adorned themselves in traditional attire, embracing the rich culture. The rhythmic beats of drums set the stage for energetic dance performances, where learners showcased their enthusiasm and traditional moves. The folk dance forms of Tamil Nadu, Karakattam, and Theru Koothu, captivated everyone with the essence of the region's cultural heritage. The significance of the day was highlighted where the learners celebrated thanking the Sun God, Mother Nature and farm animals that help to contribute to a bountiful harvest. It was a day brimming with joy, unity, and the warmth of Pongal traditions.

School Events Oct 03, 2023

NAH : World Habitat Day

This week's Nest Advocacy Hour featured Ms. Nina Subramani, a Sustainability Practitioner, Climate Activist, Producer at Riverbank Studios in New Delhi, Filmmaker, and Founder of Elephant Corridor. The discussion centered around the theme of rapid urbanization and its consequences for the environment and human poverty, in recognition of World Habitat Day. Ms. Nina Subramani highlighted how Chennai's once-rich landscapes have transformed into a bustling urban metropolis over time. During the session, Ms. Nina Subramani emphasized various small-scale sustainable practices that individuals can incorporate into their lives, ranging from minimizing food waste to reducing the consumption of new clothing. It was inspiring to note that Ms. Subramani practices what she preaches, using a bicycle for her everyday transportation, serving as a powerful source of motivation for the participants.

Krishna Janmashtami
