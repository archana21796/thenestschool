PRIMARY

Led by the International Baccalaureate (IB) curriculum, the primary years at The NEST School focus solely on the holistic development of every learner. We aspire to help develop internationally minded individuals through an inquiry-led curriculum, enabling them to express their ideas, views and questions. Emphasis is on inculcating 21st century skills through authentic, engaging and relevant curriculum and an inquiry-led facilitation of the same.

Reimagining Learning

Real World Applications

Future readiness and enhancement of skills essential for the learner’s future is organically developed in The NEST School Primary Years Programme. Our syllabus, Programme of Inquiry (POI) is developed keeping these requisites in mind, in addition to providing ample scope for learners to be agentic and internationally minded.

Identifying Talents

Driven by a strong desire to identify, develop and strengthen individual pursuits and talents, The NEST School is committed to this purpose. We offer extra support for learners with exceptional talents and for learners who want to excel in a particular field of interest. Academic expectations are met through differentiated instructions and modified learning expectations for the learner by prioritising learners’ efficacy.

Community Outreach

A culture of shared guardianship of the planet is inculcated by raising societal consciousness and instilling enthusiasm to lead a sustainable life. The NEST School Advocacy Hour, The NEST School Dives, Organic Farming Hour amongst other sustainability endeavours are incorporated as part of our daily life at The NEST School.
