Admissions

At The NEST School, we believe every child deserves a joyful and nurturing start to their learning journey.
Our admissions process is designed to be simple, transparent, and supportive, ensuring parents feel confident every step of the way.

We welcome students from Pre-KG to Grade 10. Admissions are open to all children who meet the age and eligibility criteria for their respective grades. Our academic year begins in June, and applications are accepted throughout the year based on seat availability.

Attend the Personal Interview

After qualifying in the interaction / written assessment, shortlisted applicants will be invited for a personal / online interview before a decision on their application is taken. Admission decisions will be communicated to the parent through an email from the school office.

REMIT THE SCHOOL FEE

After confirmation of admission, school fee shall be remitted within three days from the date of acceptance. Fee details can be obtained from the school office. In case of cancellation/withdrawal of admission by the parent for whatever reason may be, the fee paid shall not be refunded.
