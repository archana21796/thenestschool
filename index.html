To Parents looking for a World Class Education in Chennai for their Children,

At The NEST School, we view education as a transformative journey, one that nurtures lifelong learners and encourages children to understand and explore their unique selves. It is imperative that this journey is filled with joy and tailored to the individual needs and interests of each child. In line with our vision we adopt the IB PYP program in our Primary years and a Cambridge program for our Lower Secondary Program. As a leading IB School in Chennai, our PYP program commits to an educational philosophy that places the child at the centre of all our endeavours.

We recognize and celebrate the individuality of each child, understanding that they are endowed with innate talents waiting to be discovered. The conventional 'one size fits all' approach to education is obsolete and irrelevant in today’s times. As a premier Cambridge School in Chennai, our Middle School plays a crucial role in guiding each child to self-discovery, especially in a world brimming with uncertainties like climate change, resource scarcity, and shifting societal norms. Our mission is to equip our learners with creative problem-solving skills to navigate these challenges.

Our commitment is to prepare our children for an unpredictable future. This means fostering an insatiable quest for knowledge, igniting curiosity, and empowering them to take charge of their learning. Aspiring to be one of the best schools in Chennai, The NEST School is built on several foundational pillars designed to develop critical, creative, and compassionate thinkers. Our educational approach is dynamic, experiential, and engaging, utilizing a variety of platforms, tools, and techniques to ensure the effectiveness of our learning environment. We are dedicated to crafting a holistic educational experience that shapes the confident and capable 'Gen NEST'—the next generation of leaders, innovators, and thinkers.

As you consider school admissions for your child, we invite you to explore the difference an international school in Chennai like The NEST School can make. Join us in our commitment to providing a world-class education that prepares your child to thrive in an ever-evolving global landscape.

The NEST School is a leading IB and Cambridge school in Chennai providing a stimulating and nurturing environment for students focusing on their academic and holistic development. Our curated curriculum follows the International Baccalaureate (IB) Primary Years Programme for grades Pre KG - Grade 5, followed by the Cambridge Lower Secondary Programme and Cambridge IGCSE for Grades 6-10.

Grade   Program
Pre-KG   An inhouse curriculum adapted from the IB PYP
LKG – Grade 5   International Baccalaureate Primary Years Programme
Grades 6 to Grade 8   Cambridge Lower Secondary Programme
Grades 9 & 10   Cambridge IGCSE
Grades 11 & 12   Not currently offered

Admissions are open for the academic year 2024-25 for the grades Pre-KG to Grade 9. We are a renowned international school in Chennai, offering diverse curriculums like the IB Primary Years Programme and Cambridge Lower Secondary Programme for younger learners, alongside IGCSE.

The NEST School, a leading international school in Chennai, places student safety as its top priority. We rigorously adhere to all building, electrical, fire, and sanitation safety standards. Our secure campus features CCTV surveillance at all locations, monitored continuously throughout school hours, ensuring peace of mind for parents. Additionally, we offer a convenient and secure drop-in/drop-out process powered by a card scanning system, further enhancing campus safety for all our students.

We currently do not offer lunch services at the school.

Committed to both international standards and continuous improvement, The NEST School recruits IB-qualified and enthusiastic educators who embrace international-mindedness, flexibility, and creativity. We invest in their professional development through dedicated programs and workshops, including those for maintaining IB certification. This dedication ensures your child receives an education delivered by the best and brightest in the field.

The Cambridge Lower Secondary (CLS) at The NEST School is designed in a way to provide a balanced programme to our learners and help them to have a seamless transition into IGCSE

Starting from Grade 6, we provide elective courses with options such as STEM, Finance, Literature, and Self-Management each semester. Our program also incorporates Life Skills and CAS (Creativity, Activity, Service) to ensure a well-rounded educational experience.

The NEST School embraces the magic of languages. We build a strong foundation in English while welcoming the beauty of Tamil, Hindi, and French into our Early Years classrooms. This early exposure sparks curiosity and lays the groundwork for language acquisition. Starting in Grade 1, parents can choose a second language for their child, empowering them to delve deeper and develop linguistic mastery. This commitment to multilingualism sets The NEST School apart as a leading international school in Chennai, offering a curriculum that prepares students for a global future where communication bridges cultures and opens doors to endless possibilities.

We expect learners to choose one language from the above stated list and the learners will be offered support to learn the language

At The NEST School, physical education is not fragmented, but integrated into different subjects. Learners are trained in the rules and strategies of various track and field events. While the number of sessions are grade dependent we try to ensure that learners will get at least 3 lessons in physical education per week.

Art, Dance, Music, Yoga are some of the co-curricular activities that we have brought in as compulsory aspects in our curriculum. Apart from this we have an in house curriculum developed for Sustainability, Finance and Information and Communication Technologies.

Yes, we currently offer sports as part of our afterschool program, including cricket, skating, and basketball. Additionally, we plan to introduce more activities in the near future.

We understand that some families looking for cambridge schools in chennai might have concerns about transitioning from the IB program. Rest assured, The NEST School's curriculum is meticulously designed with this transition in mind. Through backward mapping methods, we guarantee seamless alignment between the IB Primary Years Programme and the Cambridge Lower Secondary curriculum, ensuring a smooth and enriching learning experience for your child.

ICSE is a national level exam offered in India by CISCE (The council for the Indian school certificate examinations) while IGCSE is an international standard curriculum offered by CAIE, UK. IGCSE grades students based on a three-tier grading system.

IGCSE is known for its practical and application-based approach, allowing learners to choose courses aligned with their interests in science, humanities, languages, and mathematics. The curriculum incorporates components of fine arts, visual arts, theatre, music, community service, etc., providing a diverse educational experience. Emphasising critical thinking, analytical skills, communication, investigation, problem-solving, teamwork, and independent research, the syllabus prepares students with qualities highly valued by international universities. With a broader range of subjects, IGCSE fosters all-round development and offers a more challenging academic experience.
