Admission Procedure

  • STEP 1

    Enquiry

    Submit an online enquiry form or mail us at enquiry@thenest.school or contact our admissions office at 9940106378 for an appointment.

    1/4
  • STEP 2

    School Visit and Interaction

    Take a school tour and meet our Admissions Team along with your child to understand our culture, values and philosophy. We conduct an interaction and/or Prior Knowledge Assessment to understand the learner better.

    2/4
  • STEP 3

    Application

    Fill up the admission form, send in all documents and pay the fee. Admit letter is given once all procedure is complete.

    3/4
  • STEP 4

    Join

    Welcome to The NEST Family! Your child is ready to take The NEST Step

    4/4
