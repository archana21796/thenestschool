PRIVACY POLICY

The Nest School, 363, Arcot Road, Kodambakkam, Chennai-600024 (“we,” “us”) shares the concerns of visitors (“Visitors,” “you”) to our Internet world wide web site (the “Site”) that links directly to this privacy policy about the protection of personal information online. This privacy policy (the “Policy”) describes our practices regarding the privacy of information that we collect through the Site, which is hosted and operated from the India. This Policy does not govern our collection of information through any web site, or by any other means, other than through the Site, or the collection of information by any parent, subsidiaries, sponsors or affiliated companies (“Affiliated Entities”).

The protection of your personal data during collection, processing and use when visiting our website pages is a matter of importance to us.

Some data, which is saved on our server, is data you have given us voluntarily by completing the forms while other data is collected automatically. We do not collect any data from which the identity of each individual visitor can be derived, unless you transmit your personal data to us yourself.

This privacy policy is applicable to the present Site and not to any other Site to which you may be lead to by means of links provided herein. In case of other such websites, privacy policies applicable to those websites are applicable.

The Nest School may amend this privacy policy at any time in its sole discretion without prior notice to you. All copyrights, trademarks, trade names, logos acknowledged.

Automatically collected and saved data

We track the general use of the website (Performance Measurement) in order to improve it for our visitors and to adjust it to their requirements. If necessary, we automatically collect the following data about your computer and your visit during your visit and save this data temporarily:

  • Domain and host from which you use the internet
  • If applicable, the internet address of the website from which you accessed this website by a link
  • Date and time of connection with our website, duration as well as selection of the visited pages
  • Internet protocol (IP) address of your computer and
  • If applicable, operating system and the browser software of your computer
  • Further personal data such as your name, address, phone number or e-mail address are not collected unless you give them voluntarily,

Use and disclosure of personal data

As far as you provided us with personal data, we use it only for technical administration of our websites and for the fulfillment of your requests and requirements or in order to answer your enquiry. Your personal data is not disclosed to a third party, unless this is necessary for the fulfillment of your requirements. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. We may also release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others rights, property, or safety.

Cookies, routines, active contents

If you visit our website, information may be saved on your computer by way of so-called cookies which enable us to identify your computer automatically during your next visit of our homepage. Cookies enable us among others to adjust a website to your desires or to save your password in such a way that you do not have to enter it again each time. If you do not wish our recognition of information on your computer, please set your internet browser in a way that it deletes cookies from your hard disk, blocks cookies or warns you prior to saving a cookie. If you leave our page via a link and thus access foreign pages, cookies may also be saved by the addressee of the clicked page. We are not liable for such cookies. Please check the privacy policy of our advertising partners concerning the use of such cookies and the information saved therein.

Security advice

We endeavor to take all necessary technical and organizational security measures in order to save your personal data in a way that it is neither accessible by third parties nor by the public. If you wish to contact us by e-mail, please note that the confidentiality of the information transmitted via this communication means is not completely ensured. We therefore recommend sending confidential information only by post When you voluntarily send us electronic mail, we will keep a record of this information so that we can respond to you. We only collect information from you when you register on our site or fill out a form. Also, when filling out a form on our site, you may be asked to enter your: name, e-mail address or phone number. You may, however, visit our site anonymously. In case you have submitted your personal information and contact details, we reserve the rights to Call, SMS, Email or WhatsApp about our products and offers, even if your number has DND activated on it.

Contact Information

We welcome feedback regarding this privacy policy. If at any time you believe that this web site has not adhered to this Privacy Policy, please contact us and we will use all commercially reasonable efforts to promptly determine and correct the problem.
