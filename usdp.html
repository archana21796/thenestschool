Empowering Global Minds: Dual Diploma Programme at The Nest School

The Nest School offers a unique dual diploma programme that combines the strengths of the Cambridge International Examinations (CIE) A-Level and the US High School Diploma Programme (USDP). This innovative programme allows students to achieve both the AS/A-Level diploma and the US High School Diploma within the same four-year timeframe, providing a comprehensive and globally recognized education.

Key Features of the Dual Diploma Programme
  • Eligibility: Open to students who have completed Grade 8 with an aggregate score of at least 80% and are proactive high achievers, initially focusing on the Science and Math stream in Grade 11.
  • Course Structure: Students must complete six specific courses with AGS between Grades 9-12, alongside their CIE pathway requirements. This includes IGCSE and AS/A-Level exams to complete the Cambridge pathway.
  • Flexibility and Support: The programme offers flexibility in learning, with students able to continue the CIE pathway even if they choose to drop the USDP. Dedicated mentors provide support to ensure progress and help overcome challenges.
  • Advantages: Students can benefit from completing both diplomas within the same timeframe, allowing them to take advantage of university-level courses for credit transfer to US universities. This dual diploma enhances their academic profile, providing a competitive edge in international admissions.
By offering this dual diploma programme, The Nest School empowers students to achieve academic excellence, flexibility, and global recognition, setting them up for success in an increasingly interconnected world.
