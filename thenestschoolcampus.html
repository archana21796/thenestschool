The NEST School Campus

Imbibing sustainability at its heart, our campus is designed for open spaces, a harmonious fusion with natural elements and as a place for your child to explore and pursue their passions – to nurture a generation of holistic young learners. Want to explore the campus yourself? We’d love to schedule an appointment here for you.

Signature Highlights

  • Multi-Purpose Auditorium (1000-seater) – for indoor sports, events, performances, and community gatherings
  • The Sky Stage – an open-to-sky, courtyard-style amphitheatre with seating for 500 members
  • The Discovery Hub – our reimagined Library: a sanctuary for imagination, exploration, and the joy of reading
  • Campus-wide state-of-the-art CCTV security

Learning Hubs

  • 40+ Collaborative Smart Classrooms – technology-enabled, flexible spaces for interactive learning
  • Indoor–Outdoor Classrooms – designed especially for our energetic Early Years learners

Collaborative & Reflective Spaces

  • Multi-Purpose Auditorium (1000-seater) – for indoor sports, events, performances, and community gatherings
  • The Mound – a vibrant campus lung where ideas breathe and collaboration thrives
  • The Huts – cozy corners for informal learning, reflection, and connection
  • The Terrarium – a living space where students collaborate, explore, and discover through nature

Innovation & Discovery Labs

  • Advanced Labs – Coding, AI/ML, Robotics, Languages & Communication, Ethical Hacking, ICT
  • Composite Skill Lab in partnership with AeroBay – hands-on innovation in aerospace, drones, robotics, and sustainability
  • State-of-the-art Science Labs – Physics, Chemistry, and Biology labs that bring Science to life

Creative & Community Spaces

  • Creative Studios – Visual Arts, Performing Arts, Graphic Design, Theatre, and Dance
  • Wonder Room – celebrating inclusion and learning diversity
  • Student Corner – a dedicated student-led engagement zone
  • Facilitator Lounges – for quiet reflections
  • Parent & Visitor Lounge – a warm space for connection and conversations

Sustainability in Practice

Children actively participate and experience our sustainable practices through:

  • Multi-Purpose Auditorium (1000-seater) – for indoor sports, events, performances, and community gatherings
  • Solar Thermal Plants
  • Solar PV Plants
  • Sewage Treatment Plants
  • Composting Yards
